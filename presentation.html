Performance Summary

This analysis compares the dealership's performance between February and May, highlighting the impact of Fountain Forward's advertising efforts on CORE Website Leads.

Key Metrics (May vs February)

  • Total Cars Sold: +189.4% (47 to 136)
  • Total Gross Revenue: +864.7% ($11,963 to $115,407)
  • Average Gross per Car: +233.4% ($254.54 to $848.58)
  • Cost Per Car: -67.3% ($1,227 to $401)
  • Cost Per Lead: -63.1% ($160.61 to $59.28)
  • Total Profit: +864.7% ($11,963 to $115,407)

Performance Metrics (May)

  • Lead Conversion Rate: 14.8% (up from 13.1%)
  • ROI: 211.4% (up from 20.7%)
  • Total Marketing Spend: $54,595

Sales Funnel Analysis

The sales funnel shows the conversion from initial leads to final sales. The ROI improved by 190.7 percentage points (from 20.7% to 211.4%), with significant improvements in cost efficiency and lead conversion.

Conversion Rates

Key metrics show significant improvement, with lead conversion rate increasing from 13.1% to 14.8% and ROI improving from 20.7% to 211.4%. The cost per lead decreased by 63.1% to $59.28.

Financial Performance

Total profit increased by 864.7% from $11,963 to $115,407 from February to May, demonstrating the effectiveness of the marketing strategy.

Cost Analysis

Marketing cost per car sold decreased by 67.3%, from $1,227 to $401, while cost per lead decreased by 63.1%, from $160.61 to $59.28, indicating much higher efficiency in marketing spend.

Average Gross per Car

The average gross profit per car sold increased by 233.4%, from $254.54 to $848.58, indicating higher value transactions and better deal structuring.
